How it works

The app loads the CSV from data/Iris.csv, splits the rows into train and test sets, then predicts with a simple k-nearest neighbors model.

  • No build step or external dependencies
  • Uses the real Iris dataset included in the repo
  • Shows accuracy after an 80/20 train-test split
